We are Frigora. An events band form the Philippines. With years of experience in the industry, our pool of musicians specialize in adding that special touch of music to weddings, private parties, and corporate events. Covering and arranging songs from last decade's soulful music to disco party music from the 80's to yesterday's top R&B hits, we know for sure our acoustic or full band teams are the perfect entertainment for your next event.
